Whats New!

  • New School Canteen

    Our new canteen is made with high-quallity architecture. With modern tables and chairs, your child will feel comfortable while eating. We have done a health inpection and our food is perfectly safe for your kids to eat. We have 5 new lunch options: pizza, mac & cheese, orange chicken, fruit punch, and sugar cookies. We have hired some new chefs to make wonderful lunches for your kids! We have lower all food prices to $0.50 for your conveince. We have aimed to make our kids happier with their lunch as last year many had complained about the quality. We hope we hit the target!


  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    We, as people, make mistakes. Maybe we forgot to tell you school was out, or forgot to give attendance. We have fixed that. May we present, IOMS or Implemented Online Attendance Management System. This is an accurate attendance program which will make sure we know if your child is here or not.
    How it works? You might be thinking, "How does this wonderouse program work?" Well, we have an answer. On the TV screen in each classroom, there will be an attendance announcement. It will ask for every student's name and if they are here. They have up to 20 seconds to answer. 'Here' is the word to check your child's name off. If it isn't said, they will be counted missing. 20 seconds should be enough time to say the word 'here'. This only takes about 10 min of their school time. After atendance is taken, you will get a notification that your child is there. You may choose between using out app with data on how your child is doing, or email for the notifications.
    Thank you! This was only possible with the generous donations all the St. Xaiver familys have given. All the money goes to city and school benifits. So don't forget to donate you contibution. This may only be done in person until our online pay has been set up. Once again, thank you very much! We couldn't have done this without you guys!
